About Us

The company Swaghili Sports Limited, which aims to deliver sports material and advance the sports marketing agenda to the Kajiado, Kenyan, and international markets, is home to the fully integrated brand Football Made In Kajiado. We are Kajiado County's sole source for accurate and dependable news regarding players, transfers, and all grassroots sports news. We are dedicated to giving our audience members unprecedented access to the best sporting events and entertainment, as well as inspiring tales that reflect our culture. Our goal is to recognize, highlight, develop, and provide educational opportunities to young people through soccer.
